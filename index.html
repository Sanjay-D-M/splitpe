How it works

Three words in WhatsApp. Done.

01
💬

Add to your group

Add @SplitPe to any existing WhatsApp group. No one needs to sign up. No app install. It just works in the chat your group already uses.

02
⚡

Whoever paid types: @splitpe ₹800 Rohan paid Zomato. That's it. SplitPe reads the message and calculates everyone's share automatically.

A card appears in the chat with each person's amount and a one-tap UPI link. No chasing. No screenshots. Settled before anyone forgets.

The old way

Why Splitwise doesn't actually work here

  • Everyone needs to download a separate app
  • Someone has to manually enter every expense
  • "Remind" notifications feel passive aggressive
  • Settles in foreign currency (not built for UPI)
  • Dead after 3 days — group forgets to use it

The SplitPe way

Lives inside the chat. Zero friction.

  • Zero downloads — it's a WhatsApp bot
  • One message = split done, automatically
  • Friendly reminders inside the same group chat
  • Native UPI deeplinks — pay in one tap
  • Group already uses WhatsApp — adoption is built in

The playbook

How we go from 0 to 100K groups in 6 months

Week 1 — Validate

Does anyone actually want this?

  • Post in 5 Bangalore FB/Reddit groups: "Would you use this?"
  • Manual WhatsApp bot (you do the math, reply manually)
  • Goal: 50 real users, track if they come back
  • Talk to 20 users, record what they say out loud
Week 2–4 — Build MVP

Make it work, not perfect

  • WhatsApp Business API bot (Twilio / WATI)
  • Parse "₹X [name] paid [reason]" command
  • Auto-generate UPI deeplinks for each member
  • Basic group ledger with running balances
Month 2–3 — Grow

Let the product spread itself

  • Every bill message = "Powered by SplitPe" watermark
  • Referral: invite a new group, get 1 month Pro free
  • Target PGs, hostels, engineering colleges directly
  • Partner with Zomato/Swiggy group-order flows
Month 4+ — Monetize

Revenue that users don't resent

  • Free tier: unlimited splits, basic reminders
  • Pro ₹49/mo: recurring expense tracking, analytics
  • 0.1% cut on UPI transactions we facilitate
  • B2B: offices, co-living spaces, travel groups
